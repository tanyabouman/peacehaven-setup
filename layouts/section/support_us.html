How you can help

  • If you have special interests or skills and a heart for Christian service, we would love to hear from you.
  • Check the services page and consider how you can help
  • Remember Peace Haven in your prayers

Volunteer

  • Providing supports to persons living at Peace Haven to be contributing members of their community, enjoy recreational activities and to find work placements.
  • On call tradesmen to help out with maintenance work.
  • Assist at our Day support program at “The Farm” with social and recreational activities.
  • Assist with special events.

For more information and how you can be involved, please contact the Director of Peace Haven.

Financial Support

  • Send an e-transfer. For a donation receipt to be issued, please include your address information in the message line.
  • Make a cheque
  • Donate through your church (see your deacons)
All donations are tax deductible.
